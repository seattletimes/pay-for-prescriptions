Did your doctor take company cash?

An analysis by ProPublica, with cooperation from The Seattle Times, provides evidence of a link between drug-firm and medical-device payments to doctors, including more than 3,000 in Washington.

This searchable list includes doctors in five common medical specialties who prescribed more than 1,000 prescriptions for Medicare patients in 2014. Doctors who prescribed drugs at a high rate compared with their peers are marked with ●. Those who prescribed drugs at a very high rate compared with other doctors in their field are marked ●●.
